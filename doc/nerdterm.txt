		Toggle Terminal Window



								*CTRL-`*
:NERDTerm							*:NERDTerm*

Start or toggle visibility of the terminal window.


It is possible to override the CTRL-` mapping by mapping to
`<Plug>(NERDTermToggle)`.  For example, to change the mapping to CTRL-|: >

	:noremap <C-\|> <Plug>(NERDTermToggle)
	:noremap! <C-\|> <Plug>(NERDTermToggle)
	:tnoremap <C-\|> <Plug>(NERDTermToggle)
<
